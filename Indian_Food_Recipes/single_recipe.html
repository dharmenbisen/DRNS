Crispy and Easy Aloo Bread Patties

Aloo bread patties is a very easy and simple recipe. You can serve this as evening snack with a cup of hot masala chai. This is a popular street food that tastes just awesome. Everybody will love this. It is a must try recipe in rainy season.

Prep time

30 min.

Cook time

15 min.

Serving

6 Servings

Tags : Veg (1) Breakfast (2) Non-Veg (3) Sweets (4)

Instructions

Step 1

Take green chilies, garlic in a blender jar and blend everything into coarse paste. Thecha is ready.

Step 2

Heat up oil in a pan and hing, curry leaves, thecha and fry well.

Step 3

Turn off the gas and add turmeric powder and mix well.

Step 4

Add potato, salt, coriander and mix well.

Step 5

Remove the curry leaves and stuffing for bread pattice is ready.

Step 6

Take besan, rice flour, salt, baking soda, red chili powder, turmeric powder in a bowl and mix everything well together.

Step 7

Add a little water at a time to make a medium consistency batter.

Step 8

Add coriander and mix well. Batter is ready.

Step 9

Take bread slice and cut it into 2 rectangles.

Step 10

Spread potato stuffing and press it a little..

Step 11

Dip the bread into the batter and coat it well.

Step 12

Heat up a pan on medium heat and drop the patties into hot oil.

Step 13

Heat up a pan on medium heat and drop the patties into hot oil. When one side is fried well flip the patties over and fry well from other side too.

Step 14

When patties is fried well from both sides take it out, drain excess oil and transfer it into a dish.

Step 15

Bread patties is all ready.

ingredients

10~12 green chilies

10~12 Garlic cloves

2 tsp Oil

1/4 tsp Hing

Curry leaves

1/4 tsp Turmeric powder

Boiled mashed Potato

Salt to taste

Finely chopped Coriander

1 cup Besan

1/4 cup Rice flour

Salt to taste

A pinch of Baking soda

1/2 tsp Red chili powder

1/4 tsp Turmeric powder

1/2 cup Water

Bread slices

Oil for frying
